Abilities

DEMON SHADE

When out of combat, Evelynn enters Demon Shade.
Demon Shade heals Evelynn when she is low on health and grants Camouflage after level 6.

HATE SPIKE

Evelynn strikes out with her Lasher, dealing damage to the first unit hit.
Then, Evelynn can shoot a line of spikes at nearby foes up to 3 times.

ALLURE

Evelynn curses her target, causing her next attack or spell after a delay to charm her target and reduce their magic resist.

WHIPLASH

Evelynn whips her target with her Lasher, dealing damage.
She then gains Move Speed for a short duration.

LAST CARESS

Evelynn briefly goes untargetable and decimates the area in front of her before warping backwards a long distance.
