Abilities

SALVATION

Soraka runs faster towards nearby low health allies.

STARCALL

A star falls from the sky at the target location dealing magic damage and slowing enemies.
If an enemy champion is hit by Starcall, Soraka recovers Health.

ASTRAL INFUSION

Soraka sacrifices a portion of her own health to heal another friendly champion.

EQUINOX

Creates a zone at a location that silences all enemies inside.
When the zone expires, all enemies still inside are rooted.

WISH

Soraka fills her allies with hope, instantly restoring health to herself and all allied champions.
