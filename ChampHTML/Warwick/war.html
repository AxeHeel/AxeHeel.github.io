Abilities

ETERNAL HUNGER

Warwick's basic attacks deal bonus magic damage.
If Warwick is below 50% health, he heals the same amount.
If Warwick is below 25% health, this healing triples.

JAWS OF THE BEAST

Warwick lunges forward and bites his target, dealing damage based on their maximum health and healing for damage dealt.

BLOOD HUNT

Warwick senses enemies below 50% health, gaining Move Speed toward and attack speed against them.
When they fall below 20% health, he frenzies and these bonuses triple.

PRIMAL HOWL

Warwick gains damage reduction for 2.5 seconds.
At the end, or if re-activated, he howls, causing nearby enemies to flee for 1 second.

INFINITE DURESS

Warwick leaps in a direction (scaling with his bonus Move Speed), suppressing the first champion he collides with for 1.5 seconds.
